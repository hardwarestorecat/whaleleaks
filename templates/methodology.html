What is whaleleaks?

whaleleaks monitors real-money prediction markets (Polymarket) in real time and surfaces addresses that consistently place large, high-upside bets that resolve in their favor. The goal is to identify wallets that appear to have informational advantages — insider knowledge, superior research, or political access — and track their moves as they happen.

Whale Alert Criteria

A trade must meet both of the following thresholds to appear as a Whale Alert:

Minimum Spend
≥ $3,000
USD value placed on a single trade (size × price)
Minimum Potential Profit
≥ $1,000
Net gain if the bet resolves correctly (payout − cost)

This filters out large bets on near-certain outcomes (e.g. $20k on a 98¢ favourite) where the upside is trivial. We only care about bets where someone is risking real money on a meaningful opinion.

Early Exits (Realized P&L)

Prediction markets allow traders to sell their position before a market resolves. When an address sells shares at a higher price than they paid, we capture this as a realized win — no need to wait for market resolution.

  • We track average cost basis per address per market token
  • On a SELL: realized P&L = (sell price − avg buy price) × quantity sold
  • Wins are credited immediately, not at resolution time
  • Early exits meeting whale criteria also generate a Whale Alert tagged EXIT

Graduated Whales

An address that accumulates ≥ $10,000 in total realized gains over 30 days becomes a Graduated Whale. Graduated whales receive enhanced tracking regardless of per-trade size:

  • Every trade from a graduated whale is saved to the long-term database
  • Smaller trades that don't meet the $3k / $1k thresholds are still recorded and visible
  • Graduation status rolls on a 30-day window — it can be lost if recent P&L drops below the threshold

Win Rate & Leaderboard

The Top Whales leaderboard ranks tracked addresses by two methods:

Primary — By Win Rate

Addresses with at least 3 resolved bets and ≥ $1k cumulative P&L, ranked by win rate (wins ÷ resolved bets), then by total P&L as a tiebreaker.

Fallback — By Total Earned

If fewer than one address qualifies for the win-rate board (not enough resolved bets yet), the leaderboard falls back to all addresses with ≥ $1k total P&L, ranked by cumulative earnings.

What counts as a "win"?

  • RESOLVED WIN Market resolves in the direction they bet
  • EARLY EXIT Address sells their position at a profit before resolution

What does NOT count?

  • PUSH Markets declared invalid — cost basis returned, no win/loss recorded
  • Bets below $500 are excluded from win-rate calculations
  • Sports markets are tracked in the flow feed but excluded from whale alerts

Data Sources

  • Polymarket — data-api.polymarket.com/trades polled every 3 seconds
  • Market resolution — gamma-api.polymarket.com polled hourly
  • No authentication required. All on-chain data is public.

What This Is Not

  • Not financial advice
  • Past win rates do not guarantee future performance
  • Addresses with high win rates may be market makers, arbitrageurs, or simply lucky — not necessarily insiders
  • We do not know the identity of any address
